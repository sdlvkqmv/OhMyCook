OhMyCook is an AI-powered recipe assistant application that recommends personalized recipes based on the ingredients users currently have. Going beyond simple recipe listings, it leverages Google Gemini AI to provide the following intelligent features:

* **Smart Recipe Recommendations**: Suggests customized recipes tailored to the user's available ingredients and specific cooking preferences (e.g., difficulty, cook time, spiciness, cuisine type).
* **Receipt Analysis**: Analyzes receipt images to automatically extract and register purchased food ingredients into the user's inventory.
* **AI Chef Chat**: Features an AI Chef chatbot that kindly answers cooking-related questions, comprehensively considering the user's cooking skill level, available kitchen tools, and allergy information.
